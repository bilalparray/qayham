Overview

Built for real-world use

NetDrop sends photos, videos, and documents directly between devices on the same network. No cloud middleman, no account, and no QAYHAM file servers — just pick a file, pick a device, and transfer.

Local network transfers

Files go straight to the device you approve on your LAN.

You stay in control

Decline transfers you do not want and choose what to send.

Organized saves

Received files land in sensible folders on your device.

Features

Device discovery

Find NetDrop devices nearby using multicast on your network.

Progress tracking

Transfer history
