Overview

Built for real-world use

QR Scanner & Generator handles both sides of QR workflows: read codes in a flash with your camera, or create new ones for Wi‑Fi networks, URLs, contacts, and more. Core scanning works offline so you are covered even without data.

Fast scanning

Point your camera at any QR or barcode and get results instantly.

Code generator
