Overview

Built for real-world use

MeshChat uses Bluetooth to connect devices in the same space. Message friends at events, camps, or anywhere signal fails — without creating an account or sending your chats through QAYHAM. Your conversations stay between devices.

Nearby messaging

Discover and chat with devices running MeshChat in Bluetooth range.

No account required

Pick a display name locally — no email or phone number needed.

No cloud relay

Messages travel device-to-device, not through our infrastructure.
