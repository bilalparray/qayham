Overview

Built for real-world use

Trade Journal helps you log every session, track profit and loss, and review what actually works — not what you wish worked. From quick daily entries to deep strategy reviews, it keeps your edge sharp and your data organized across devices.

Structured trade logging

Capture dates, P&L, market type, tags, psychology notes, and screenshots in one place.
