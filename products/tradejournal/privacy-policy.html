1. Who we are

TradeJournal is a trading journal and daily profit & loss tracker. The App is published under the developer name Qayham. For privacy-related questions, contact us at parraybilal34@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide and data generated by your use of the App:

Account information

  • Display name, email address, and profile photo (including when you sign in with Google)
  • Account identifier assigned by our authentication provider
  • Preferences such as currency and theme
  • Subscription status (free or Pro), if applicable

Trading journal content

  • Trade entries: dates, profit/loss amounts, number of trades, market type, notes, tags, psychology ratings
  • Trading strategies you create or use

  • Personal journal entries and mood
  • Trade screenshots and profile photos you choose to upload

Device and usage information

  • App interaction events (for example, screens viewed and entries created), via analytics

  • Notification preferences and scheduled reminder settings you configure in the App
  • Purchase-related information when you subscribe to Pro (processed by Google Play; we store subscription status)

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and sync your data across devices
  • Store and display your trading journal, analytics, calendar, and goals
  • Provide Pro features such as cloud sync, CSV import, and extended storage
  • Process in-app subscriptions through Google Play Billing
  • Send local notifications you enable (for example, daily reminders)
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance of the App

We do not sell your personal information.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
