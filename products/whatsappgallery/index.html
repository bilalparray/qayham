Overview

Built for real-world use

WhatsApp Gallery turns scattered status downloads and chat media into a clean, searchable gallery. Browse photos and videos saved by WhatsApp, preview instantly, share anywhere, and optionally save to your camera roll — without uploading anything to our servers.

Instant media discovery

Find WhatsApp photos, videos, and documents already stored on your device.

Beautiful previews
